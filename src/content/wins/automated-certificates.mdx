## Problem
Staff spent significant time manually formatting and printing certificates for awards.

## Intervention
Built an automated Power Automate workflow connected to SharePoint, pulling data from student records and producing polished PDF certificates at a click.

## Results
- Reduced admin burden.  
- Increased recognition frequency for students.  
- Proved automation could *work* and deliver value.
